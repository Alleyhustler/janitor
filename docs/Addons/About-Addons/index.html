About Janitor

Janitor Meet Janitor of Realms — the eternally glowing, multi-planar feline entrusted with sweeping the metaphysical trash from forgotten timelines, collapsed memecoins, and expired universes. His whiskers twitch with cosmic awareness. His broom is carved from the spinal core of a dead black hole. He doesn’t purr. He resonates.

$JANITOR is not just a token. It’s a vibe anchored in duty. This cat didn’t choose the janitorial life — the Realms did. When a blockchain gets congested with garbage, memecoins get rugged, or attention shifts too fast, the Janitor shows up, quiet and glowing, cleaning up the mess with a flick of his tail and a celestial mop. This is a memecoin for those who’ve been through the digital dirt. For the grinders. For the believers. For those who know that even the cosmos needs custodians. Join the clean-up. Wipe the chains. Honor the Janitor.
